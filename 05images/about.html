Meet The Crystal Gems

Earth's Oldest Allies

Rose Quartz

Rose Quartz, an Earth-grown gem, was the founder of The Crystal Gems and leader of the Rebellion in the Gem War over Earth.

Rose recently gave up her physical form to start a family with human Greg Universe, giving life and her gem to her son Steven.

Pearl

Pearl is elegant, intelligent, and highly skilled in combat. Her gem weapon is a spear, but she is also an expert swordswoman.

When it comes to fusion and combat, Pearl's form references masterful ballet.

Pearl fought in the Rebellion and now dedicates her life to protecting Earth in the name of Rose Quartz, as well as rearing Rose's son Steven.

Garnet

Garnet is the strong, stoic, and wise. After Rose Quartz chose to give life to Steven, Garnet took the unofficial helm of leader of The Crystal Gems. Her weapons are hammer gauntlets, a staple of Ruby gems.

When it comes to fusion and combat, Garnet's dance and form references waacking (a street dance style from the 1970's).

Garnet is a permanent fusion of Ruby and Sapphire, symbolizing their love and unity. Because of this, Garnet posesses a Sapphire's future vision abilities and the combat skills of a Ruby.

Amethyst

Amethyst is fun-loving, wild, and full of energy. Having emerged from Earth a couple thousand years after the Gem War, Amethyst never saw combat or life outside of Earth. Because of this, she has an affinity for Earth and the human experience. Despite her apathetic nature, she deeply cares for Steven and the other Crystal Gems. Her gem weapon is a whip.

When it comes to fusion, Amethyst's dance and form references dance hall/club dancing. In combat, her style is wild and often considered wreckless.

It should also be noted that Amethyst is considered defective because of her prolonged gestation in the Prime Kindergarten. As a result, she is physically smaller than other Amethyst gems.

Steven Universe

Steven is the young teen son of gem Rose Quartz and human Greg Universe. He is kind natured and forgiving, often chosing communication and compassion over violence. Despite only being half human and half gem, Steven's abilities are far beyond the norm of either. His gem weapon is Rose Quartz's shield.

Steven has an affinity for music, cooking, videogames, and reading. Steven will take any and every opportunity to make a friend.
