Welcome to The Crystal Gems

Who Are the Crystal Gems?

The Crystal Gems are a team of light-based beings dedicated to protecting Earth from intergalactic threats. Comprised of Garnet, Amethyst, Pearl, and young half-human Steven Universe, this powerful group defends the planet with teamwork, courage, and creative problem solving. Originally formed as a rebellion against the oppressive Diamonds of the Gem Homeworld, the Crystal Gems now stand for freedom, individuality, and the beauty of being different.

What Do The Crystal Gems Do?

  1. Fight to protect humanity from the threats of alien gems from outer space
  2. Protect humans from corrupted gems, which are gems that have been damaged and lost their original purpose
  3. Heal and rehabilitate corrupted gems and assimilate them back into society
