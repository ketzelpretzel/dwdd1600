Who Are the Crystal Gems?

The Crystal Gems are a group of magical beings dedicated to protecting Earth.

What Do They Do?

The Crystal Gems fight to protect humanity from the threats of alien gem-based beings from outer space.

They also protect the Earth from corrupted gems, which are gems that have been damaged and lost their original purpose.

When Earth isn't under attack, the Gems work together to locate, capture, and take care of these corrupted gems roaming Earth.
