Who Are the Crystal Gems?

The Crystal Gems are a group of magical beings dedicated to protecting Earth.

Main Members

  • Rose Quartz
  • Pearl
  • Garnet
  • Amethyst
  • Steven Universe

What Do The Crystal Gems Do?

  1. Fight to protect humanity from the threats of alien gems from outer space
  2. Protect humans from corrupted gems, which are gems that have been damaged and lost their original purpose
  3. Heal and rehabilitate corrupted gems and assimilate them back into society
